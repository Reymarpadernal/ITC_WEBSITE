About Us

Ann’s Kakanin is a small local business dedicated to serving classic Filipino rice delicacies made with love. Using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, we bring you the comforting taste of home in every bite. Whether for celebrations or everyday cravings, Ann’s Kakanin is here to share the sweetness of Filipino culture.

Our Story

It started when my aunt loves cooking Different dishes and eventually came up with the idea of business that literary focus just on kakanin A filipino food pastires that are affordable and accessable for all. And called it Ann's Kakanin.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, authentic Filipino kakanin that brings comfort, joy, and a taste of home to every customer. We aim to preserve traditional recipes while delivering fresh and delicious products every day.
